# Basics

A quick loop for new players. Expand this page as systems settle.

## Survive

- Keep **hunger**, **thirst**, and **health** up — berries help early, cooked meat is better.
- Gather **wood** and **stone**, then craft a pickaxe and a simple sword.
- Build a small shelter before night pressure / wildlife gets rough.

## Gather → craft → place

1. Harvest resources in the world (trees, rocks, bushes).
2. Craft tools and placeables at a **workbench** (or from recipes you unlock).
3. Place **walls**, **doors**, **fires**, and crafting stations to claim space.

## Combat notes

- Hold a weapon in the main hand for attack damage.
- Some tools double as weak weapons; swords / spears are the real fight kit.
- Hostile animals aggro differently — bees wander in, bears hit hard, deer just flee.
